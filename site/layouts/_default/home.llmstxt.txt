# Jon Seager

> Personal blog and portfolio of Jon Seager, VP Engineering for Ubuntu at Canonical.
> Writing about Ubuntu, Linux, open source, Nix, software engineering, and leadership.

Jon is a technical leader and software engineer working for Canonical as VP Engineering.
His primary focus is leading the development of Ubuntu and Juju. In his own time, he's a
keen open source software advocate with a long history of tinkering with the Linux desktop.

This site is built with Go, Hugo, and Nix. The source is available on GitHub:
https://github.com/jnsgruk/jnsgr.uk
